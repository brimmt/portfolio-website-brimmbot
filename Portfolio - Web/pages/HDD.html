Data Challenges Conquered

Missing Data (15%)

Implemented intelligent imputation strategies and data validation rules to handle missing patient information.

Date Format Chaos

Standardized multiple date formats across admission, discharge, and treatment records for consistent analysis.

Inconsistent Categories

Created standardized taxonomies for diagnoses, treatments, and department classifications.

Outlier Detection

Identified and corrected anomalous values in length of stay, costs, and patient demographics.

Data Relationships

Established proper relationships between patient records, treatments, and outcomes for accurate analysis.

Data Privacy

Implemented proper anonymization and privacy protection measures for sensitive healthcare information.

Key Insights Discovered

Operational Insights

  • Peak admission times and seasonal patterns identified
  • Average length of stay varies significantly by department
  • Cost optimization opportunities in resource allocation
  • Staffing patterns correlation with patient outcomes

Clinical Insights

  • Treatment effectiveness patterns by condition type
  • Readmission rates correlation with discharge planning
  • Medication adherence impact on recovery times
  • Specialist consultation timing affects outcomes
