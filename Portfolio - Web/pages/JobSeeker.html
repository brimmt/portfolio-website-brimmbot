What Is Job Seeker AI?

Smart Resume Builder

AI-generated resumes tailored to specific job descriptions, industries, and career levels with intelligent keyword optimization and formatting.

Personalized Cover Letters

Custom cover letters that match company culture, job requirements, and your unique background - generated in seconds, not hours.

Job Search Strategy

Intelligent job matching and search strategies based on your skills, experience, and career goals with market insights and recommendations.

Career Analytics

Track application success rates, identify improvement areas, and get data-driven insights to optimize your job search approach.

Why I'm Building Job Seeker AI

Job searching is stressful, time-consuming, and often inefficient. I want to change that with intelligent automation and personalized guidance.

Time Efficiency

Job seekers spend hours customizing resumes and cover letters. AI can do this in minutes while maintaining quality and personalization.

Better Targeting

Most applications are generic. AI can analyze job descriptions and tailor applications to match exactly what employers want.

Reduce Stress

Job searching is emotionally draining. Having an AI assistant provides support, guidance, and confidence throughout the process.

Planned Technology Stack

AI & Backend

  • Python + FastAPI - Backend framework
  • LangChain - AI workflow orchestration
  • Multiple LLMs - GPT, Claude, and more
  • MCP - Model Context Protocol

Frontend

  • HTML5 - Semantic structure
  • Tailwind CSS - Modern styling
  • JavaScript - Interactive features
  • Responsive Design - All devices

Data & Storage

  • Supabase - Database and auth
  • PDF Generation - Resume exports
  • Email Integration - Application tracking
  • Analytics - Success metrics
